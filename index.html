Riset & Inovasi Maritim

Riset dikembangkan berbasis permasalahan industri dan visi teknologi masa depan, mengacu pada standar IMO MASS Code [1] dan penelitian AI maritim global [2] [3].

Fokus Riset Utama

  • Maritime Autonomous Surface Ships (MASS)
  • AI-powered navigation & collision avoidance [2]
  • IoT sensors untuk monitoring kapal [4]
  • Predictive maintenance systems [3]
  • Green shipping & emission reduction
  • Smart port automation
  • Cyber security untuk sistem kapal

Kolaborasi Global

MASS & Autonomous Maritime System

Pengembangan sistem kapal cerdas berbasis sensor fusion, AI, dan kendali otomatis sesuai IMO MASS Framework [1]. Market AI maritim diproyeksikan tumbuh 23% per tahun hingga 2029 [6].

Teknologi MASS

  • Degree 1-4 autonomy (IMO standard)
  • Remote Operations Centre (ROC)
  • AI-based decision support systems
  • Sensor fusion & computer vision
  • Autonomous navigation algorithms

Aplikasi IoT Maritime

  • Real-time vessel tracking & monitoring [4]
  • Predictive maintenance sensors
  • Environmental compliance monitoring
  • Safety & security systems
  • Fuel optimization analytics
